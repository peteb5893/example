About

My name is Peter Bennington and I am a 22-year-old student from Northern Ireland, studying Applied Computing at the University of Dundee, Scotland. This September, I begin my 4th and final year of university, with the aim of achieving a 2:1 BSc Honours degree classification. After graduation I plan on pursuing a career in web or app development.

Outside of my academic activity, I enjoy exercising and playing sports. I have been playing hockey competitively for over a decade now, first for teams in Banbridge, but since I moved to Scotland, I play for Dundee University Men's Hockey Club. As well as the university competition (BUCS), DUMHC are also a club team, playing in Scottish National Division 3.

Another interest of mine is film and television. I like a wide variety of genres, but I would have to admit my favourite movie genre is action and adventure. Despite never reading comic books as a kid, I am loving the Marvel superhero movies. I also love TV shows like Sons of Anarchy, The Big Bang Theory and Parks and Recreation.

Travelling is another passion of mine. I would love to see every country in the world and experience their cultures. Four years ago, I took part in the Lions International Youth Exchange in Belgium and I had such a great time that I went again the following year, to Denmark. At first, I was worried about going somewhere for 3 weeks without knowing a single person there, but now I've made friends from all over the world and I hope to visit each of them again sometime in the future.
